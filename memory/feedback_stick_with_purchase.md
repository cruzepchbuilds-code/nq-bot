---
name: feedback-stick-with-purchase
description: Do not suggest switching providers or alternatives after the user has already committed to buying something
metadata:
  type: feedback
---

After the user purchases something, do not suggest alternatives or second-guess the choice — even if a "better" option exists. The user already committed money and time. The cost of switching again is higher than any marginal improvement.

**Why:** User explicitly called this out twice: first with the LucidDirect account, then with the Contabo VPS. Both times Claude suggested switching after the purchase was already made or in progress.

**How to apply:** Once a purchase is confirmed or in progress, execute on that choice. If there's a genuine blocker (not just "this other thing is slightly better"), raise it once clearly. Otherwise commit to making the current choice work.
